## Tips for Flip Phone Users

### Make Navigation Easier

1. **Use the Up/Down buttons** in the links menu to navigate
2. **Tap directly on links** if you prefer touching the screen
3. **Use Back button** frequently to return to the menu

### Optimize for Your Flip Phone

1. **Larger text**: The app automatically uses larger buttons and text for flip phones
2. **Easy scrolling**: Use the touchscreen to scroll webpages
3. **Hardware buttons**: All main functions have dedicated buttons

### Best Practices

1. **Keep your links list short** - easier to navigate with buttons
2. **Use clear website names** - easier to find what you want
3. **Test new websites** - make sure they work on your flip phone screen
4. **Export your list regularly** - backup your websites

## Troubleshooting

### Website won't load
- Check that you have internet connection
- Make sure the URL is correct
- Try pressing "âŸ³ Reload"

### Can't see the buttons clearly
- Adjust your screen brightness
- Make sure you're holding the phone correctly
- The buttons are designed for flip phone screens

### Back button doesn't work
- Make sure you're not in a text input field
- Try pressing it again
- If stuck, close and reopen the app

### Links won't scroll
- Use the "â–² Up" and "â–¼ Down" buttons
- Or scroll with your finger on the screen
